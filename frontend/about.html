Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Srilanka's trusted bus booking platform

Connecting Srilanka, One Journey at a Time

Founded in 2020, BusBooking was born from a simple vision: to make bus travel across Srilanka more accessible, comfortable, and reliable. We recognized the challenges faced by millions of travelers who depend on bus transportation for their daily commutes and long-distance journeys.

Our platform bridges the gap between passengers and bus operators, providing a seamless booking experience while ensuring transparency, safety, and convenience. Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers across multiple cities, connecting communities and making travel dreams a reality.
